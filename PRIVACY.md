# Privacy Policy

**Ocular** is a free local search engine. Your privacy is simple: your data stays with you.

## What Ocular Accesses

- **Google Drive (optional):** If you connect your Google Drive, Ocular requests read-only access to download and index your files. This is done entirely within your browser.
- **Local Files:** Ocular scans folders you select on your computer to extract and index text content.

## How Your Data Is Handled

- All file processing and text extraction happens **locally** — either in your browser or on your own machine.
- Ocular does **not** send your file contents, search queries, or personal data to any external server.
- No analytics, tracking, or telemetry is collected.
- No data is sold or shared with third parties.

## Data Storage

- Indexed text is stored locally in a SQLite database on your machine and in your browser's IndexedDB.
- Google Drive OAuth tokens are held in browser memory for the duration of your session and are not persisted.
